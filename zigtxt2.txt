GitHub's CEO said young engineers frequently bring along fresh perspectives.
They're more likely to have been early adopters of AI, in particular, Thomas Dohmke told "The Pragmatic Engineer."
You still have to develop "craft" in engineering, the CEO added, but AI will form part of the process, going forward.
In the age of AI coding, GitHub's CEO says he's still a believer that junior engineers are of great value to tech companies. Just don't be surprised if you're asked to showcase your prompt-engineering skills in a job interview.
